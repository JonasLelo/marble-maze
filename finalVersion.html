Marble Maze

Roll the marble through dangerous labyrinths.

How To Play

🎮

Controls

Use arrow keys, WASD, mouse drag or device tilt to move the marble.

🌀

Goal

Reach the glowing spiral to complete the maze.

🕳️

Holes

Falling into a hole respawns you at the start.

💡

Tips

Move carefully and control your speed in corners.
