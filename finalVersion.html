How To Play

🎮 Controls

Tilt your device or use arrow keys to move the marble.

🎯 Goal

Reach the glowing spiral at the end of the maze.

🕳 Holes

Falling into holes resets your marble to the start position.

💡 Tips

Move slowly near corners and avoid building too much speed.
